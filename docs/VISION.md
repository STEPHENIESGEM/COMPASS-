# COMPASS- : Vision Document
## The North Star

**COMPASS-** exists to answer one question: What if the greatest game ever made also made you a master?

We are building the world's first AR + AI powered 3D Digital Twin Auto Shop Simulator -- a fully immersive platform where players learn real motorcycle and automotive mastery through gameplay, not memorization.

---

## The Problem We Solve

Millions of people ride motorcycles, own vehicles, and dream of working on machines -- but the path to mastery is locked behind:
- Years of expensive trade school
- Geographic barriers (no shop nearby)
- Time barriers (cant stop working to learn)
- Cost barriers (tools, parts, mistakes are expensive)
- Confidence barriers (fear of breaking something)

**COMPASS- removes every one of these barriers.**

---

## The Solution

A living, breathing 3D Digital Twin of a full auto shop -- powered by AI that knows everything about every vehicle ever made -- rendered in AR so learners can overlay the virtual shop onto their real garage, their real bike, their real world.

---

## The Mission

> To make world-class mechanical mastery accessible to every human being on the planet, regardless of geography, income, or background.
